The ITU Radiocommunication Assembly, 
considering 
a) 
that selective-calling in the shore-to-ship, ship-to-ship and ship-to-shore directions would 
expedite the handling of traffic in the maritime mobile service; 
b) 
that the International Maritime Organization (IMO) has listed a number of operational 
requirements that should be taken into account when designing a general purpose selective-calling 
system; 
c) 
that Chapter IV of the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea (SOLAS), 1974, 
as amended, requires the use of digital selective calling (DSC) for distress alerting and safety calling 
in the global maritime distress and safety system (GMDSS);
